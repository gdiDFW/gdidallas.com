Classes & Materials

GDI uses nationally cultivated curriculum with modifications made by each class teacher. Each chapter might have some differences in class offerings, so email us at chicago@girldevelopit.com or fill out our interest survey if you don’t see what you’re looking for! If there’s enough interest, we’ll work to get it started for you. We're always looking for feedback to tailor our offerings to the needs of our community

Intermediate HTML & CSS An 8-hour workshop covering HTML5, CSS3, responsive design, etc.

JS203: Client-side APIs A 3.5-hour workshop covering multiple types of client-side APIs
JS204: Object-Oriented JavaScript A 4-hour workshop with group and individual exercises for OOP in JavaScript
JS205: MVC Frameworks A 4–4.5-hour workshop that covers the basics of MVC, how to write your own, and common types of MV* patterns

Career Development Series

Our career-focused events are always changing, so keep an eye on the Meetup page for awesome upcoming events. Some of the past career-related events we've hosted include:

Salary Negotiation Workshop Workshop discussing salaries, other cash compensation, company equity including stock options, shares and other lesser known equity types, negotiations & how to be strong and confident during a time of fear & uncertainty.
A Day In the Life Series Hear from a panel of ladies from different companies and at different points in their career about how they got to where they are, what their day-to-day responsibilities are like, and what advice they have for folks looking to enter the field.
Professional Heashots Need an updated photo for your LinkedIn profile, personal site, or just want to have a nice, non-selfie on hand? Join GDI Chicago for a special professional headshot session with an experienced photographer at an affordable price.
Resume Writing & Portfolio Workshop This is a workshop for GDI members to get two 30-minute one-on-one expert reviews of your Portfolio & Resume that will help you stand out in your career search.
Lightning Talks Join us for a series of lightning talks with folks from all walks of life as they speak on a variety of topics. GDI members are welcome to apply to speak at any of the lightning talk events.
How to Make "Networking"­ Work Better and Suck Less Practical tips for networking, from starting a conversation at a Meetup to following up in a productive, collaborative manner.
Mentor Me Join us for an opportunity to meet and mingle with mentors in the Chicago dev community!
