Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sign my daughter up?

Despite our name, our events are geared for women 18+, and for legal reasons, we cannot accept younger women into our event.

Fortunately, however, there are other opportunities for young girls to learn programming and technology:

  • Code & Cupcakes A beginning coding workshop for mothers and daughters in Chicago
  • Coder Dojo CoderDojoChi is a volunteer not for profit that teaches web, game, and app development to youth ages 7 to 17

Can men attend classes or volunteer to help out?

Yep! Anyone over 18 is welcome to attend classes & volunteer to TA, we just ask that all attendees, including staff and volunteers, abide by the Girl Develop It Code of Conduct.
