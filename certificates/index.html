Below are the GDI Certificate Tracks we are offering. At the end of completing all of the required classes in each track, the student will receive a GDI certificate proving your mastery in the track's topic(s). The student will also have the option of accepting an internship or job at several DFW companies.

(It will be up to you, the student, to acquire the certificate from the leaders of GDI DFW. Please email dallas@girldevelopit.com to inquire about your certificate)
